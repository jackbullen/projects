Co-op Job Search Automation

Streamlining the job application process with web scraping and AI-driven cover letter generation.

Overview

Modern job hunting, especially in the competitive co-op space, requires an edge. This project combines the power of web scraping to identify fresh job listings and the AI capabilities of GPT-4 to generate compelling, personalized cover letters on-the-fly.

How It Works

  • Job postings from major co-op job boards are scraped daily to identify new listings.
  • Relevant details like the job title, company name, and job responsibilities are extracted.
  • Using the extracted data, GPT-4 tailors a unique cover letter for each position, emphasizing the applicant's strengths and alignment with the job description.
  • Users receive a notification with the job details and the generated cover letter, ready for submission.
